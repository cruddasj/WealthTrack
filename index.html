Welcome aboard

This stripped-down shell keeps the navigation, card layout, theme controls, and settings experience from the production template. Use it as a launchpad for your next progressive web app by replacing these cards with the content your project needs.

Layout highlights

  • A familiar card grid ready for your onboarding steps, dashboards, or quick links.
  • Update controls now live in the settings view, keeping the welcome screen focused on onboarding content.
